This is not a course list. It is a cathedral — built proof by proof, poem by poem, to a standard no one is watching me keep.

Logic teaches honesty. Quantum teaches strangeness. Poetry teaches precision. Together they make you dangerous — the kind of thinker who can prove the completeness theorem in the morning and write something true about it by night.

Six months from now there will be thirty proofs, a portfolio, and an application to the programs that matter. This page exists so that on the hard days, the reason is never more than one click away.
